Why this matters for the Satoshi mystery: Cryptographic signing with a private key doesn't prove identity — it proves control. But control of the genesis block keys would be, by orders of magnitude, the most compelling evidence of an association with Satoshi Nakamoto. No amount of computing power — not every computer on Earth running until the heat death of the universe — could forge those keys. That's why if someone were to sign a message with the genesis block's private keys, every hypothesis door swings wide open.
