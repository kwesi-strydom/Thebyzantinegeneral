# Contributors

## People who have contributed to the research

Every accepted contribution is credited here. Contributors choose how they want to be attributed - real name, pseudonym, GitHub handle, or wallet address.

---

### Founding Researcher

**The Byzantine General** - Project creator and lead researcher. Initiated the investigation in 2018 and maintains the editorial direction.

---

### How to appear here

Submit a contribution via [GitHub pull request](https://github.com/kwesi-strydom/Thebyzantinegeneral). Once your contribution is reviewed and merged, you'll be added to this page with your preferred attribution.

Your attribution can be:

- A real name
- A pseudonym
- Your GitHub username
- A wallet address (ETH or BTC)
- A social media handle (X/Twitter, etc.)

You can change your attribution at any time by opening a PR.
